Electricity Consumption in Karnataka

Karnataka, being one of the leading states in India, has a significant electricity consumption profile. The state's power consumption is influenced by various factors including industrial activity, agricultural needs, and urban development. In recent years, there have been efforts to improve the efficiency of electricity use and incorporate sustainable practices to meet the growing demands.
