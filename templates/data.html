Electricity Consumption in Karnataka

This webpage provides insights into the electricity consumption patterns in Karnataka across different districts over several years. By analyzing historical data, we aim to forecast future energy usage trends and help in planning and managing energy resources efficiently.

Summary of the Dataset

Year: The year in which the data was recorded.

District: The district in Karnataka where the data was collected.

House Size (sqft): The size of the house in square feet.

Household Size: The number of people in the household.

Energy Consumption (kWh): The amount of energy consumed in kilowatt-hours.

Price (in Rupees): The cost of energy consumption in Rupees.

Report

Data Description

The dataset provides detailed information on energy consumption patterns across different districts in Karnataka. The data spans multiple years, giving insights into how energy consumption has evolved over time. The key variables include:

  • Year: Indicates the year of the observation, helping to understand temporal trends in energy consumption.
  • District: Represents the geographic location within Karnataka, aiding in analyzing regional differences in energy consumption.
  • House Size (sqft): Measures the size of the house, which can be correlated with energy consumption.
  • Household Size: Indicates the number of people in the household, impacting energy usage.
  • Energy Consumption (kWh): The primary variable of interest, showing the amount of energy consumed.
  • Price (in Rupees): Represents the cost of the consumed energy, used to analyze the economic aspects of energy usage.

Preliminary Analysis

  • Temporal Trends: Identify trends in energy consumption over different years, understanding factors like technological advancements, population growth, and economic changes.
  • Regional Analysis: Analyze data for different districts to identify regional variations in energy consumption for targeted policy-making and resource allocation.
  • Correlation Analysis: Examine the correlation between house size, household size, and energy consumption to understand the factors driving energy usage.

Potential Insights

  • Predictive Modeling: Build predictive models using historical data to forecast future energy consumption, aiding in better planning and management of energy resources.
  • Policy Recommendations: Provide recommendations for improving energy efficiency and reducing consumption based on the analysis.
  • Cost Analysis: Study the economic impact of energy consumption by analyzing the price data, suggesting ways to make energy more affordable.
